The Mission

This site was created for my final project in a web development course. It's a showcase of what I’ve learned: semantic HTML, CSS styling, responsive design, and JavaScript interactivity — all wrapped in a fun, superhero-inspired theme.

Why Batman?

Batman is the ultimate blend of brains and brawn. He’s a detective, a strategist, and a vigilante wrapped in shadows and gadgets. That neon-noir vibe? All inspired by the Dark Knight.

  • Iron Man: Tech genius with a heart of gold (literally in an arc reactor).
  • Scarlet Witch: Reality-bending sorceress with immense emotional depth.
  • Captain America: The moral compass of the Marvel universe — a true leader.
